Shoyu Chicken

Description:

Shoyu chicken is a delicious and easy-to-make dish that combines the flavors of soy sauce with tender chicken.


Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated

Steps:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Add the soy sauce, garlic, and ginger. Stir to combine.
  4. Cook for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
